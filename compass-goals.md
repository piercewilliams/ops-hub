## Goals

### Goal 1 — Product Liaison & Pipeline Request Management (25%)

**Organizational objectives aligned:** Operational Excellence, Accountability, and Ethical Practice • Innovation at Scale and Platform Acceleration

**SMART statement:** Serve as the primary interface between the programming and product/technology teams by maintaining a fully documented request register with actionable context at all times, reducing average fulfillment time quarter-over-quarter by Q3, and ensuring zero open requests go unaddressed for more than 30 days throughout 2026.

**Key measures:**
1. 100% of open requests documented with actionable context at all times
2. Avg fulfillment time on closed requests decreasing QoQ by Q3
3. 0 open requests unaddressed >30 days without documented status
4. 100% of new pipeline variations reviewed against quality standards before adoption

**Milestones:**
- Q2: Request register fully operational; baseline fulfillment time established
- Q3: At least 50% of current open product requests resolved or in active dev
- Q4: Fulfillment time lower than Q2 baseline; 0 requests dark >30 days

**Milestones:**
- Q2: QC checklist and framework documented; error rate baselines established
- Q3: QC process running on all new launches; differentiation and metadata rates being tracked
- Q4: Variant differentiation ≥50%; metadata completeness ≥90%
