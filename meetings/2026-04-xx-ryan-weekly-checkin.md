## Purpose
Weekly touchpoint with Ryan (Trend Hunter team, reports to Sara Vallone) to review keyword and writing tool outputs, surface friction, and gather enhancement feedback. This is a lightweight feedback loop — not a project meeting. The goal is to build a reliable signal about whether tools are working for the people using them, and to catch mismatches between what Pierce is building and what the team actually needs.

## Prep for Pierce
- Review any new keyword briefs or tool outputs produced that week
- Note any open questions about Sara's team's usage patterns
- Have a short list of the 1–2 specific things you'd most want Ryan's take on

## Standing Agenda (30 min or less)

### 1. Tool output review (~15 min)
- Walk through any keyword briefs or tool-generated content from the past week
- What's landing well? What feels off or unhelpful?
- Are there recommendations Ryan's team is acting on? Ignoring? Why?

### 2. Friction log (~10 min)
- What's currently annoying, slow, or missing?
- Any workflow step the tool could be helping with that it isn't?
- Any output quality issues Pierce should know about?

### 3. Enhancement ideas (~5 min)
- Is Ryan hearing anything from Sara about what she'd want to see differently?
- Anything from Ryan's own use that should be flagged for a future build?

## How to Use These Notes
Log any action items back to the relevant project cards in ops-hub. Ryan's feedback is the primary signal for whether the keyword tool and future agentic writing helpers are moving in the right direction before wider rollout. If something comes up repeatedly over 2–3 check-ins, it's a build priority.

## Notes
- Ryan works for Sara — this is a field-level feedback channel, not a stakeholder alignment meeting
- Do not use this time to introduce new features or pitch ideas — listen first
- Log patterns to data-keywords/CONTEXT.md and write-assist/CONTEXT.md as applicable
